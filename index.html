Talent Match


The Talent Match Reverse Recruitment Fair is a great opportunity to find the candidate or job of your dreams here in Berlin. Accelerate the recruitment process by finding and interviewing for candidates and jobs on the very same day.

WHEN: Friday 20th November 2015, 10:00 - 18:00

WHERE: Project A, Zinnowitzer Str. 8, Berlin, 10115

Powered by Eventbrite

Agenda

10:00

Doors open to candidates to set up their tables and have breakfast. Opening remarks from organizing team.

11:00

Doors open to companies. Top level sponsors talk for 30 mins. Companies meet candidates and arrange interviews

13:00

Lunch Break

14:00

Interviews Begin

15:30

Closing remarks from organizing team

16:00

Beers & networking

18:00

Fair closes. After Party


What will happen?

  • Candidates will set up tables displaying their skills
  • Companies will come around, meet fantastic candidates and interview them

What do I need to do to participate?

  • Candidates: have a project, put down a deposit to reserve your spot, you must be living in Berlin or willing to relocate
  • Companies: buy a ticket, you must be able to prove you can pay a living wage to your hires and you must be hiring in Berlin

Why do developers love it?

  • You get the chance to demonstrate your practical skills, more than a resume
  • You get to interview with companies on the day
  • You get to meet interesting companies that you have never heard of, but that we have screened to make sure they have the money to pay you
  • Get to interview with companies on the day

Why do companies love it?

  • You will have the chance to, in-person, screen some of the best developers that are actively looking for jobs in Berlin right now
  • Candidates come in from all around the world
  • Candidates are prescreened to make sure they are highly skilled and can demonstrate it with a project
  • You'll meet candidates that want to work for companies just like yours, but have never heard of you

Why does everybody love it?

  • Networking with like minded people in Berlin
  • There will be beer and a party afterwards
